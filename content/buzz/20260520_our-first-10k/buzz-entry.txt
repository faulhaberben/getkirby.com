Title: Our first €10,000 in donations

----

Category: News

----

Intro: In 2024, we added a tiny little checkbox to our checkout form that made a huge difference …

----

Text:

That's when we started donating €1 to a good cause for every license sold, and the checkbox lets customers add another €1 to match our contribution. This month, we hit a major milestone: together with our customers, we've collected €10,000 in donations. This is a great result and a huge motivational boost to continue with this program.

Over the past two years, we've donated to three different causes, switching whenever it felt right. To find them, we turned to the German organization (link: https://effektiv-spenden.org/ text: Effektiv Spenden) ("effective donations") which evaluates NGOs working in areas such as climate change, poverty reduction, and the protection of democratic values. It identifies those that really make a difference and use donations most effectively.

Here is our donation history:

- 2024 - (link: https://www.amadeu-antonio-stiftung.de text: Amadeu Antonio Foundation): €3202
- 2025 - (link: https://www.againstmalaria.com text: Against Malaria Foundation): €5533
- 2026 - (link: https://fcarchitects.org/ text: Future Cleantech Architects): €1279 (still running)

This milestone would not have been possible without our customers' generous support. We look forward to supporting more good causes this way.
